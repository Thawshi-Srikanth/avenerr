## Description

Our Information Systems form the backbone of our operations, enabling accuracy, efficiency, and regulatory compliance across the organization. We utilize integrated digital platforms to support inventory control, batch and expiry tracking, documentation management and real-time operational reporting. These systems facilitate seamless coordination between Regulatory Affairs, Stores, Logistics, Marketing and Management, ensuring transparency, traceability and adherence to NMRA requirements and Good Distribution Practices (GDP).
