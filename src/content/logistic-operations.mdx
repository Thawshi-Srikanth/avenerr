## Description

Our Logistics Operations are structured to ensure the safe, reliable, and timely distribution of pharmaceuticals and surgical instruments throughout Sri Lanka. Supported by compliant storage facilities and systematic warehouse management, our Stores and Logistics teams strictly follow Good Storage and Distribution Practices (GSP/GDP). From product receipt and controlled storage to order processing and final delivery, every step is carefully monitored to preserve product quality, integrity, and availability to healthcare institutions.
