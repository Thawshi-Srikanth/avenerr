## Description

Regulatory compliance is embedded in every aspect of our operations. Our Regulatory Affairs and Quality teams ensure that all products, processes, documentation, and promotional materials fully comply with Sri Lankan NMRA regulations and applicable international standards. Through rigorous review, monitoring, and cross-department collaboration, we uphold quality, safety, and ethical standards across our supply chain and commercial activities.
