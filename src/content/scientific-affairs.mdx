## Description

Our Scientific Affairs function plays a vital role in ensuring responsible, ethical, and evidence-based engagement with healthcare professionals. The team provides accurate medical and product information, supports regulatory documentation and facilitates scientific communication aligned with NMRA guidelines. Working closely with Marketing and Regulatory Affairs, Scientific Affairs ensures that all promotional activities remain compliant, credible, and focused on patient safety and clinical value.
